Nope! We want to fill our studio with photo samples of real people. You don’t have to be a model to qualify, either. We want to see real smiles and real connections between moms and their kids in our photos.


What will I get?

You get a complimentary professional photography session, valued at $300, plus a $100 gift voucher just for signing up, and another $100 gift voucher for keeping your original session date and time, for a total value of $500!


Can I bring family members too?

Of course! We are happy to accommodate siblings, parents, grandparents, fur babies, and anyone you would want to be photographed with!


But what should I wear?

No worries! We’ve got you covered with the photo guide we will send you after booking your session. Meanwhile, pop on over to our Pinterest board that we’ve curated especially for you!



Can I buy photo prints from this session?

Absolutely! We want you to have photo evidence of your amazingly fun photo session! You can use your gift voucher and retainer fee towards the purchase of prints, products, wall art, and digital files.


Why is there a retainer fee for a free photo session?

Good question- We put a lot of time and effort into planning each and every photo session, and unfortunately, some people decide to not show up to their scheduled appointment time. The retainer fee will be refunded to you when you show up for your session. There is absolutely no obligation to purchase anything or use this retainer fee towards purchases.
