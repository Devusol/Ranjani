These are the faces you'll meet at Ranjani Groth Studios

RANJANI

Wanderlust is imprinted on my heart. I’ve lived in more than a dozen places across the globe, yet wherever I call home, my camera is always at my side. As a teen, I’d drag a chair into the Illinois woods and convince my sisters to wear prom dresses for photo shoots with our mom’s old film camera. I found connection with my hosts in Tajikistan, though we didn’t share a language, through the lens of my camera. In college, I studied photojournalism and worked for more than a decade as a wedding photographer. All those couples taught me how to be an expert wrangler, problem solver (I always carry a mini-sewing kit now) and mood lightener. I pivoted to studio portraits five years ago, and haven’t looked back. Today, I l o v e the happy chaos of everyone (plus the dog!) piling in for a group photo. The interaction there—between grandmas and grandkids, moms and sons—is just brimming with life.

BRADLEY

While Ranjani’s in the studio creating the whole experience, you can find me knee-deep in spreadsheets and admin duty. (Hyperfocus is like my ADHD superpower.)
I’m not all focus though. I’ve got some real loveable labrador energy that you’ll get to experience with some sweet dad jokes and my ability to make babies laugh. (Also a superpower? I think so.) Ask Ranjani how I attempted to woo her. It involved a bike ride and breaking my collarbone in front of her. True love! I promise to be more careful when you’re in the studio.

Here's what studio means today


Myth - You need to dress formally
Reality - Absolutely not! You feel your best when you’re in your favorites, so wear ‘em! That can be as formal (or informal!) as you’d like. In fact, we’ve had families who let their kids come in costume, and it was a ton of fun for everyone.


Myth - You’re a number on a calendar
Reality - Not here you’re not. Every single member of our studio is here to help you feel welcome, comfortable, and cared for. You are our priority from the moment we connect.


Myth - Studio lighting is harsh
Reality - We have years of studio experience and technical expertise to use lighting to our advantage. Take a look at our galleries to see the way we work with light to create warm images with depth and emotion.


Myth - Stiff posing and frozen smiles, eek!
Reality - Of course we always make sure to capture at least one image of everyone smiling at the camera. But we’ve found that your favorites are the ones where you’re simply interacting with your people—comfortable, natural + authentic.


BEFORE

Our goal is to intuitively answer any questions before you even have them! We start with a questionnaire to get to know you and your family. We love hearing about your lives, hopes you have for your images, and what you like to do as a family! It helps us personalize your entire session. You can also expect our custom photo guide, with tips on how to prepare, helping your kids feel included, dressing ideas, along with local places we think you’ll love.



DURING

When you arrive for your session, we’ll start with a laidback chat and get to know each other. This is a great way to get comfortable, especially for younger kids who may need to ease into the session. During your session, you’re never expected to freeze and hold a smile. We’ll guide you through the entire process, but we want you to feel like yourselves. That means warm interaction, natural movement and genuine emotion. The average session, including photos, editing and viewing, is two hours.



AFTER

After your photo session, you have some time to chill on one of our comfortable couches (there are plenty of toys for kids too) with a coffee, a snack or a book. Within an hour, we’ll present the best of the best images from your session on a large screen. Instead of expecting you to wade through gobs of photos on your own, we’ve expertly selected the images we think you’ll love. Together, we’ll pick the final images and guide you through choosing how to display them in your home.



PETS + KIDS


Yes, we know they’re not the same, but sometimes their needs are. (Hey there, toilet emergencies :) ) You can count on us to include your children in the entire process, so they feel engaged and free to be themselves. With pets, we’re always excited to introduce ourselves with a little snuggle and a treat. And when they have an accident in the studio (don’t worry, it happens), we’ll be right there to clean it up and get back to our session.

How about it? Ready to see what modern portraits are all about?

Our black and white portraits are equal parts timeless and modern. They harken back to the classic portraiture of your grandparents, great-grandparents … yet they’re modern enough to complement any design in your home.


We’re not just creating images, but a little piece of your heart preserved on the walls of your home.

Photography is an investment. That’s why guiding you through the process is a huge part of what we do. We never dump photos on you and expect you to choose, print, and place them in your homes yourselves. Let us do it for you!

We’ll present the very best images from your session and help you visualize where they’ll fit in your home with special design software. After you choose, our team will thoughtfully edit to make sure each image looks like you, but at your very best. Then we’ll work with our hand-picked producers to create your art and deliver it to your door. You can expect art on your walls within six weeks! (We don’t know about you, but that’s way less time than it takes us to remember that we’d planned to print out the photos living on our phones.)

Matted Prints
STARTING AT $700

High quality, archival prints with elegant matting are created in an eco-friendly photo lab in Italy. These matted images use Lustre paper for a rich, true-to-life print. Our matted prints come in two sizes:


  • 8x10 image in an 11x14 mat
  • 11x14 image in a 15x18 mat
Photo Boxes
STARTING AT $2,250

Our 11x14 portrait boxes are hand-crafted of beautiful Italian leather and display one 8x10 matted image at a time. The anti-scratch, acrylic lid functions as a frame, making our photo boxes a gorgeous way to preserve many images and display a variety throughout the year. Photos boxes can hold 10, 15, 20, 25 or 30 matted prints.


  • 8x10 image in an 11x14 mat
  • 11x14 image in a 15x18 mat
Canvases
STARTING AT $1,850

Heirloom quality canvases are a thoroughly modern way to hang art on your walls. Our canvases are created in Italy with an expert eye toward craftsmanship, clean design, and an anti-glare finish. Canvases are easy to hang and come in two sizes:


  • 24x36
  • 30x40
Acrylics
STARTING AT $1,850

Sleek acrylics present your images on crystal clear glass and offer lots of customization. We work with a small business based in Seattle that runs the entire process in-house to create vibrant, fade-free acrylics that will appear to “float” against your wall and be lit from within. Our acrylics can be created with single images or as collages:


  • Single image 24x36 or 30x40
  • 5-image collage 60x30
  • 11-image collage 40x40
What about digital files?

We’re big believers in the power of printed photography … but that doesn’t mean we’re over here pretending digital doesn’t exist. We offer high-res digital files to purchase, which is a great option for sharing on social media or printing on your own.
